Shawarma

Description

The Shawarma is a food that has its origins in the middle east and is loved by many around the world. It consists of a bread that is the wrap, containing various things such as veggies, meat, and sauces.

Ingrediments

  • Bread
  • Chicken/Beef
  • Tahini Sauce
  • Lettuce
  • Onions
